ii. To ensure packets are reassembled correctly, state two crucial pieces of information found in the packet header. [2]

b) New security cameras support simultaneous two-way video transmission.

i. Identify the transmission mode (Simplex, Half-duplex, or Full-duplex) used. [1]

Describe how the receiving server uses the checksum value to confirm data integrity. [2]
